About Me

I am a Ph.D candidate at HKUST, supervised by Prof. Qifeng Chen. Before PhD, I received my B.E. degree from Wuhan University. At HKUST, I mainly work on computational photography, image and video processing, and other low-level vision problems. In particular, I am interested in cutting-edge AI based sensing technologies.
